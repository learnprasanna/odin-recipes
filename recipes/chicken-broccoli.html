Chicken Stir-Fry with Broccoli

Description

This Chicken Stir-Fry with Broccoli is a vibrant and savory dish that brings together tender slices of chicken with crisp, colorful vegetables. The combination of broccoli, red bell pepper, and julienned carrots adds both visual appeal and a variety of flavors and textures. The sauce, a harmonious blend of soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, and sesame oil, elevates the dish with a perfect balance of umami and sweetness. The quick stir-fry technique ensures that the chicken remains succulent and the vegetables retain their freshness. Served over a bed of fluffy rice, this stir-fry is a delightful and wholesome meal that promises to satisfy your taste buds with every bite.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil for cooking
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice for serving

Steps

  1. n a bowl, whisk together soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, cornstarch, and sesame oil. Set aside.
  2. Season the sliced chicken with salt and pepper. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a wok or large pan over medium-high heat. Add the chicken slices and stir-fry until cooked through. Remove from the pan and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add another tablespoon of oil. Stir-fry garlic until fragrant, then add broccoli, bell pepper, and julienned carrot. Cook until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  4. Return the cooked chicken to the pan with the vegetables. Pour the prepared sauce over the chicken and vegetables. Stir everything together, ensuring the sauce evenly coats the ingredients.
  5. Serve the chicken stir-fry over cooked rice. Garnish with sesame seeds or chopped green onions if desired.

Enjoy this delicious and colorful chicken stir-fry with broccoli!
