Vegetable and Tofu Coconut Curry

Description

Indulge in the exotic flavors of this Vegetable and Tofu Coconut Curry, a comforting and aromatic dish that marries the richness of coconut milk with the subtle heat of red curry paste. Cubes of golden-brown tofu provide a hearty protein source, while a medley of vibrant vegetables adds color and nutritional goodness. The curry base, infused with sautéed onions, garlic, soy sauce, and a hint of brown sugar, creates a luscious and velvety sauce that coats each ingredient. The addition of lime juice brightens the flavors, creating a perfect harmony of sweet, savory, and citrusy notes. Served over fragrant jasmine rice and garnished with fresh cilantro, this curry promises a culinary journey through the enticing tastes of Southeast Asia. It's a vegetarian delight that will warm your soul and tantalize your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 14 oz firm tofu, cubed
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (e.g., bell peppers, snap peas, carrots)
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon red curry paste
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon brown sugar
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Cooked jasmine rice for serving

Steps

  1. In a non-stick pan, heat vegetable oil over medium heat. Add tofu cubes and cook until golden brown on all sides. Set aside.
  2. In the same pan, sauté sliced onions until softened. Add minced garlic and red curry paste, stirring until fragrant.
  3. Add mixed vegetables to the pan and cook until they begin to soften.
  4. Pour in the coconut milk and soy sauce. Stir in brown sugar and lime juice. Allow the curry to simmer until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Gently fold in the cooked tofu cubes, allowing them to absorb the flavors of the curry.
  6. Serve the vegetable and tofu coconut curry over jasmine rice. Garnish with fresh cilantro.

Enjoy this aromatic and comforting vegetable and tofu coconut curry!
